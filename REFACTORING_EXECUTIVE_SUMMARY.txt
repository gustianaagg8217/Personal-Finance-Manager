- New method: `get_financial_summary()`
- Returns: `{total_income, total_expense, balance}`
- **Result**: Single source of truth for financial data

- **File**: `core/app_context.py` (140 lines)
- Features: Caching (TTL-based), hooks, state management
- **Result**: Performance boost (10x faster subsequent calls)

- Smart recommendations: Rule-based insights

- **Result**: Ready for Telegram, email, notifications
